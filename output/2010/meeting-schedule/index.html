Meeting Schedule (Sticky)

By Ken D. Snyder, published 2010-07-22

Meeting Schedule and Topics: (all meetings run from 7:00pm - 9:00pm)
  • February 3rd, 2011 - 7:00pm - Come hang out and talk about JavaScript. We may watch some of the 2010 best JavaScript videos, talk about Google Closure Compiler, etc...
  • January 6th, 2011 - 7:00pm "Futures" by AJ. A discussion on "Declarative Component Mapping" led by Merrick. Also, Ryan may be able to show off "Element.Filters". Intro to Utah JS Challenge.
  • December 2nd, 2010 - ﻿7:00pm "MooTools" by Merrick Christensen, 7:30pm "Mobile Web Programming/Frameworks" by Jamund Ferguson. We'll be bringing Pizza again so come hungry!
  • November 4th, 2010 - Come eat pizza and discuss the current google AI programming challenge which works in most languages including NodeJS server side JavaScript. Bring your laptop (if you want), yourself and come eat pizza. Did we say we will have PIZZA?
  • October 7th, 2010 - "Open Night: Come hang out and enjoy the JavaScript talk! Be prepared to share what you are working on/excited about!"
  • September 2nd, 2010 – Dan Baker: Intro to HTML5 Canvas
  • August 5th, 2010 - Douglas Crockford Learning JavaScript - part one; Using JS Libraries; WebGL.
  • July 1st, 2010 - RingoJS, IE9 news
  • June 30th, 2010 - Hack Night! Bring code and ideas for our "asteroids" game
  • June 3rd, 2010 - Nate Pilling: "When to Use JS - Usability and Unobtrusiveness"
  • May 6th, 2010 - Ken Snyder: "Inheritance, Event Delegation and Named Function Expressions"
  • April 1st, 2010 - Kip Lawrence: "Intro to Node.js"

About UtahJS.com

UtahJS is the website for the Utah JavaScript Users Group that meets to discuss and enjoy JavaScript!

JavaScript in the browser, on the server or just about anywhere else.

We love JavaScript but quite often talk about other topics such as CSS, HTML, cross browser coding, the internet… pretty much, anything that we find interesting.

Everyone is welcome. Please join us!
