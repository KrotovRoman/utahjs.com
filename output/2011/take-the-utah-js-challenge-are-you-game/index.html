Take the Utah JS Challenge! Are You Game?

By Kip Lawrence, published 2011-01-06

In December Joyent hosted a Node.JS Camp. If, like me, you were not able to go I say we host our own! 1. Visit http://camp.nodejs.org/ and do your homework (4 steps). 2. Watch the videos. 3. Take hexes and make a game. 4. Show your creation off at the next meeting (Feb. 3rd 2011). If you have questions, revelations, etc... post to the Utah JS Challenge group.

About UtahJS.com

UtahJS is the website for the Utah JavaScript Users Group that meets to discuss and enjoy JavaScript!

JavaScript in the browser, on the server or just about anywhere else.
